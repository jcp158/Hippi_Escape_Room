Puzzle 2: Medication Lockbox

It’s time for Meadow’s Biktarvy (bictegravir, emtricitabine, and tenofovir alafenamide) but she is meditating and prefers to finish her spiritual practice before taking medications. She believes in timing doses “when her energy feels right.”

What is the appropriate action to ensure ART adherence while respecting her beliefs?

A)  Skip the dose and document that she refused.

B)  Interrupt her meditation immediately and insist she take the pill now.

C)  Gently remind her of the importance of ART timing and ask when she feels ready to take it within the next hour.

D)  Ask another nurse to deal with it since she’s being difficult.
