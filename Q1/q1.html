Puzzle 1: The Immunity Vault

Meadow’s CD4 count is 180 cells/mm³, and her ANC (absolute neutrophil count) is 0.9 x10⁹/L. She mentions she’s craving a salad with sprouts and a smoothie with raw honey from her commune garden.

Choose the Appropriate response to Meadow’s request for food, considering her immunosuppressed and neutropenic status:

A)  “That sounds delicious and healthy—I'll ask dietary to bring it to you.”

B)  “Sprouts and raw honey can carry bacteria—let’s find a safer, plant-based option you’ll enjoy.”

C)  “We’re not allowed to discuss food choices with patients.”

D)  “You should only eat hospital food, regardless of your lifestyle.”
